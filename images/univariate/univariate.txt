Univariate - Categorical Variable
1.Loan_status - Fully Paid customers are almost 6 times more than charged off.
2.Grade - Higher Grade tends to take more loan while B grade is the highest number of loan takers.
3.Experience Level (Derived Metrics) - Junior level Employees are more likely to take loans.
4.Home Ownership - People living in rent tends to take more loan
5.Issue Year - Over the years, more people tends to take loan from the company to the latest date.
6.Annual Income Range - Medium Salary People (25-50K) are more likely to take loans.
7.Loan Amount Range - Most people tends to take less amount of loan.
